    Parent Support for Autism and Developmental Needs


    Personalized coaching is available for parents of children with autism and other developmental differences. Sessions provide practical strategies for:
    • Building structure into daily routines
    • Encouraging positive behaviors while reducing challenging ones
    • Supporting communication and social skills
    • Creating sensory-friendly home environments
    • Managing stress and self-care as a parent

    This guidance blends evidence-based ABA principles with holistic practices, offering families both structured techniques and compassionate support.

    Online Art Therapy Inspired Sessions


    Art is a powerful pathway for expression, regulation, and growth. In these virtual sessions, children and teens explore creativity while also strengthening confidence, focus, and communication. Activities may include:
    • Guided drawing, painting, or mixed-media projects
    • Symbolic art to process feelings and reduce stress
    • Creative exercises that support fine-motor skills and concentration

    For children and teens who wish to expand their artistic skills, tutoring is also available in a supportive, personalized format.

    Sound Healing & Mindfulness for Kids and Families


    Sound can soothe, ground, and restore balance. Gentle sound healing practices—such as singing bowls, chimes, and guided listening, help children with autism and sensory sensitivities regulate their emotions and find calm. Parents are welcome to join, gaining tools to bring mindfulness and relaxation into daily routines at home.

    Russian Language Tutoring


    Engaging Russian lessons are offered for children and families at all levels. With a bilingual foundation in both Russian and English, sessions are interactive, playful, and culturally enriching. Instruction may include conversation practice, reading, writing, and cultural activities tailored to each learner’s goals.

    Holistic Family Coaching


    Family coaching provides a supportive space for parents to ask questions, share challenges, and learn new approaches. Drawing on experience in speech therapy, ABA, art, and mindfulness, these sessions are designed to:
    • Support families navigating new diagnoses
    • Provide strategies for smoother daily routines
    • Offer fresh perspectives on communication and behavior
    • Strengthen family harmony and resilience
